melodica is a free reed keyboard instrument that comes in 32- and 37-key models. click here for a YouTube playlist of melodica tutorials.  

baritone ukulele is a larger, lower-pitched version of a ukulele, tuned with the top four strings of a guitar. click here for a playlist of YouTube tutorials. 

bass ukulele is the same size as baritone ukulele, but with thick strings in the same range as an upright or electric bass. click here for a playlist of YouTube tutorials.

cajon is an Afro-Peruvian, box percussion instrument, based on shipping crates. click here for a playlist of YouTube tutorials.
